SWEET EXTRAS

PET RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an IAD Pet Relief Area. Located outside the Main Terminal adjacent to Baggage Claim Carousel 1 and inside on opposite ends of the Main Terminal before security. Additional areas are located inside Concourse A, across from the Virgin Atlantic Clubhouse, and Concourse D across from Gate D1.

PUBLIC ART PROGRAM

The Metropolitan Washington Airport Authority Public Art Program is featured at IAD in dozens of sculptures​, paintings, live performances and exhibits throughout the airport. Performances and exhibitions are scheduled throughout the year to enhance the traveler’s experience at IAD.

MASSAGE BAR

Enjoy a massage post security at IAD at the Massage Bar. Located in Concourse B of the Main Terminal.
