CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

Curbside baggage is currently not available for Virgin America guests at IAD.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

BUSIEST TRAVEL TIMES

The busiest travel time at IAD is the afternoon when international flights begin to depart.

SLOWEST TRAVEL TIMES

The slowest travel time at IAD is before noon.

TRAFFIC

To avoid traffic at IAD, take the Metro Silver Line to Wiehle-Reston Station. From the station, use Silver Line Express buses for service to the Main Terminal.

AEROTRAIN

IAD Aerotrain provides train service between the Main Terminal and Gates A, B and C. Service is provided to select gate departures, baggage claim and ground transportation.

AIRPORT SHUTTLE

IAD provides shuttle service between the Main Terminal and Parking Garages 1 and 2, and the Economy Parking Lot every 10 minutes daily.

SILVER LINE EXPRESS

The Silver Line Express provides bus service between IAD and Metro Silver Line via Wiehle-Reston East station every 15-20 minutes. Fares are $5 each way. WiFi and other amenities provided onboard. Service is provided on the Arrivals Level of the Main Terminal outside Door 4. Click here for schedule and additional information.

METRO

Metrobus Route 5A services transportation between IAD and L’Enfant Plaza and Rosslyn stations, and Herndon-Monroe Park & Ride. Located at Ground Transportation Curb 2E.

FAIRFAX CONNECTOR

Fairfax Connector offers daily bus service between IAD and Wiehle-Reston East Metro Station, Reston Town Center Transit Station and Herndon-Monroe Park & Ride via Route 981 and Route 983, and service to Udvar-Hazy Air and Space Museum via Route 983.

SHARED VAN

Shared van service between IAD and metropolitan destinations is available via Supreme Airport Shuttle or Super Shuttle. Reservations are not required. Counter service located at Ground Transportation Centers outside Arrivals Level Door 2 and 7.

TAXIS

Taxi service to metropolitan destinations is available via Washington Flyer Taxi. Reservations are not required. Counter service located at Ground Transportation Centers outside Arrivals Level Door 2 and 7, or call 703.572.8294.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for IAD are $30 for Terminal Hourly parking, $20 for Terminal Daily parking, $17 for Garages 1 and 2, $10 for Economy parking, $35 for the first 24 hours of Valet parking and $30 each additional 24 hours.

RESERVATIONS

None of the IAD Parking Structures accept parking reservations.

SHORT-TERM

Short-term hourly parking is available at the Terminal Parking Garage located across from the Main Terminal for $6 per hour, and $30 maximum daily. Short-term parking is also available in Parking Garages 1 and 2 for $6 per hour, and $17 maximum daily. Main Terminal access from Garages 1 and 2 is available via underground pedestrian bridges or free IAD shuttle buses.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in all Economy Parking Lots for $10 per 24 hours. Economy Parking Lots are located at Dulles Access Road and Route 28 airport exits. Long-term parking is also available in the Daily section of the Terminal Parking Garage located adjacent to the Hourly section of the garage for $20 per 24 hours.

VALET

Valet parking is available at the Terminal Parking Garage for $35 the first 24 hours, and $30 per additional 24 hours. Click here for information about Valet parking services including quick text return, dry cleaning, and car washing and detailing.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The IAD Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to those awaiting arrival flights up to 1 hour. Located at the intersection of Rudder Road and Autopilot Drive. Vehicles must be attended at all times. No parking is allowed.

DISABILITY

Designated accessible parking spaces are available at Hourly parking of the Terminal Parking Garage, Garages 1 and 2, and Blue, Green and Gold Economy Parking Lots. Click here for more information.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Four electric vehicle charging stations are located on Level 3 of Parking Garage 2. Charging station access is free with an RFID enabled credit card, Chargepoint mobile app or access card. Electricity is provided free of charge by Chargepoint. Parking rates apply.

PAY & GO

Expedite the parking process with Pay & Go. Take a parking ticket when entering a parking facility. Use this ticket to pay for parking fees at Pay & Go machines located at Terminal Parking Garage pedestrian bridges near Main Terminal east and west doors. When leaving the parking facility, use your Pay & Go ticket in designated Pay & Go exit lanes to leave parking facilities faster.
