CHECK-IN

Main terminal.

COUNTER HOURS

Open two hours prior to scheduled departure.

CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

ARRIVAL

BAGGAGE SERVICE HOURS

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for PSP are $13 for short-term and long-term parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the PSP Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

All parking is located conveniently across from the terminal building. This large lot accommodates all vehicles for passengers requiring parking before a flight, or meeters and greeters who wish to park and walk into the terminal. There are no separate lots for short-term and long-term parking. Click here for hourly parking rates.

BICYCLE

Bicycle parking is located in PSP Terminal Parking across from the terminal.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The PSP Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorists waiting for flight arrivals. Located off Kirk Douglas Parkway. Drivers must remain in the vehicle at all times while waiting for their passengersâ€™ arrival.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all PSP parking facilities. Disabled accessible parking spaces are available in the main parking lot nearest row to the passenger terminal. After arriving at the terminal front curb, remain in your vehicle. A skycap can assist with your luggage and check-in procedures. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at PSP.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle charging stations are located in PSP Terminal Parking across from the terminal.

MOTORCYCLE

Motorcycle parking is located in PSP Terminal Parking across from the terminal.
