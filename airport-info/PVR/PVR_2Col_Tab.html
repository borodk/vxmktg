LOCAL BUS

The bus stop at the Airport is located right outside by the pedestrian bridge that leads to airport taxis and the parking lot , you'll see some Resort Buses there, but look for a group of locals - they will be waiting for the bus. To get to town from the Hotel Strip or Marina take the bus that says 'Centro' and does not have 'Tunel' on it, this will take you through Downtown and to the south side terminal. The ones which have 'Tunel' bypass downtown, but still end up at the terminal on the South Side by Parque Lázaro Cárdenas. These signs are usually at the bottom of the front window. More information here.

TAXIS

Taxi and combi minivans are paid for at a small kiosk located just before exiting the terminal building. Once you have obtained your ticket, you'll be escorted or directed to your vehicle. The ticket is then handed to the parking attendant at the parking lot exit and you are on your way.
