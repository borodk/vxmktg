CHECK-IN

Jeppesen Terminal, East Ticket Counter Terminal, Level 6 (all check-in counters are on Level 6.

COUNTER HOURS

Monday - Saturday: 5:00AM - 7:00AM, 10:30AM - 12:30PM, 4:00PM - 6:00PM
Sunday: 10:30AM - 12:30PM, 4:00PM - 6:00PM

CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

Curbside check-in is not available.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

ARRIVAL

You can claim your bags at the North East Baggage Claim area. There are 3 baggage claim belts and bags can drop at 1, 2, or 3, depending on availability.

BAGGAGE SERVICE HOURS

Monday - Friday: 5:00 A.M. - 7:00 A.M., 10:30 A.M. - 12:30 P.M., 4:00 P.M. - 6:00 P.M., 9:00 P.M. - 10:00 P.M.
Saturday: 5:00 A.M. - 7:00 A.M., 10:30 A.M. - 12:30 P.M., 4:00 P.M. - 6:00 P.M.
Sunday: 10:30 A.M. - 12:30 P.M., 4:00 P.M. - 6:00 P.M. 9:00 P.M. - 10:00 P.M.

A Guest Service Teammate may not be physically present at the Baggage Service Office during the earlier hours, but should you need assistance we will have people available to help with your needs.

TSA PRE CHECK

TSA Pre✓™ expedited screening is available in: Terminal North TSA checkpoint - 4:30 a.m. to 9 p.m.; Terminal South TSA checkpoint - open 24 hours a day; A Bridge TSA checkpoint - 4:30 a.m. to 6 p.m

PROHIBITED ITEMS

Certain sharp objects, liquids, foods, and gifts are not prohibited past security checkpoints. Read the TSA's Prohibited Items list for more information about prohibited items in carry-on and checked baggage.

SHUTTLE

DEN Airport provides free shuttle between Economy parking and Main terminal. Hotel shuttles pick-up from Jeppesen Terminal Level 5, island 3, outside doors 505-507 (east side) and 510-512 (west side).

CHARTER BUSES

Different Charter bus companies provide pre-arranged services between DEN airport and several locations within Colorado. If you have arranged for charter bus transport, go to the Ground Transportation Information Counter and an agent will summon your bus from a holding lot. Charter buses pick-up from Jeppesen Terminal Level 5, island 3, outside doors 511-513 (east side) and 504-506 (west side). Click here for DEN charter buses information.

LIMOUSINES

Different limousines companies provide pre-arranged services between DEN airport and several locations within the Denver metro area and the state of Colorado. Click here for DEN charter limousines information.

MOUNTAIN CARRIERS

Different Mountain carriers provide pre-arranged, charter, and some on-demand shuttle and/or coach services to many of Colorado’s ski and resort areas. Reservations are recommended. Contact the service provider of your choice to arrange for transportation service. Mountain carriers pick-up from Jeppesen Terminal Level 5, island 3, outside doors 511-513 (east side) and 504-506 (west side). Click here for DEN Mountain carriers information.

REGIONAL TRANSPORTATION DISTRICT (RTD)

SkyRide buses pick up riders throughout the RTD service area at bus stops and at many Park-n-Rides, every day of the year. SkyRide service operates from approximately 3:30 a.m. to midnight, with the last bus leaving the airport at 1:28 a.m. RTD buses pick up and drop-off from Jeppesen Terminal Level 5, island 5, outside doors 506 and 510 (west side). Click here for DEN Public bus service information.

SHARED-RIDE SERVICES

Shared-ride service, also known as commuter shuttles, operate regularly from DEN Airport. Individual operators may provide pre-arranged, on-demand, and/or charter services to locations within the Denver metro area and the state of Colorado. Some companies may also provide service to locations outside the state. Shared-ride service shuttles pick-up and drop-off from Jeppesen Terminal Level 5, island 5, outside doors 505-507 (east side) and 510-512 (west side). Click here for DEN Shared-ride service information.

TAXIS

Taxicabs are readily available and provide service to the Denver metro area and surrounding counties. Some companies may also provide service to other destinations within the state of Colorado. DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for DEN are $96 for short-term parking, $13 - $24 for long-term Economy parking, and $33 for short-term Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

Denver International Airport offers reserved parking in both Garage East and Garage West locations for a $4 fee. Reservations can be made up to one year in advance, online, at www.DIAReservedParking.com

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available on short-term East and West parking lots. Daily parking for $96 maximum. This area is not intended for overnight parking. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Garage and Economy Parking Lots $8-24 per 24 hours. Economy and Shuttle lots are provided with free shuttle to Main Terminal. Click here for hourly parking rates.

VALET

Valet parking is available $33 daily. Valet East is located on the arrivals level (level 4) in Garage East. Valet West is located on the arrivals level (level 4) in Garage West. Click here for hourly parking rates.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The DEN Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights with restaurants, free wifi and more. Located on 7680 N. Wenatchee St. approximately three miles west of the Jeppesen Terminal.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all DEN parking facilities. All airport shuttle buses are fully accessible. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at DEN.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Ten electric vehicle charging stations are available at DEN in both the East and West Garages, Levels 1, rows E and F. Click here for more information about EV parking at DEN.
