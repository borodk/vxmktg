SWEET EXTRAS

ANIMAL RELIEF AREAS

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an HNL Animal Relief Area. Located between Overseas Terminal Garage and International Parking Garage.

LEI GREETINGS

Since the 1940s, lei makers have sold their handmade fresh flower garlands at the airport. The lei are made from many kinds of flowers but the most popular are plumeria, orchid, carnation and pikake. More information here.

HNL MEDICAL CLINIC

Receive medical services before or after your flight from the Honolulu Medical Clinic. Medical services are provided by paramedics 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Located on the ground floor of the Central Concourse next to the Japanese Garden.

ART AT HNL

The State Foundation on Culture and the Arts has commissioned a variety of art works for display at the airport. They range from free-standing sculptures to murals. You will find them throughout the terminals. You may enjoy as well HNL Cultural Gardens and other displays. Click here for more information.
