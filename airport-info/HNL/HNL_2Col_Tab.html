CHECK-IN

Located in the Overseas Terminal - Lobby 6.

COUNTER HOURS

Counter will open 3 hours prior to flight time and close 30 minutes prior to flight time.

CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

Curbside check-in is not available at HNL.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

ARRIVAL

Baggage will be available for pick up from either carousels E or F.

BAGGAGE SERVICE HOURS

There will be a Teammate up until 30 minutes after flight arrival offering baggage assistance.

ARRIVAL

TSA Pre✓™ expedited security screening is available in HNL. TSA Pre-check is available at Checkpoints 1, 3 & 5 during their hours of operation, Checkpoint 1 is open from 4:15am-11:00pm daily, Checkpoint 3 is open 24 hours and Checkpoint 5 is open from 3:15am-11:15pm. These hours are subject to change by TSA.

PROHIBITED ITEMS

Certain sharp objects, liquids, foods, and gifts are not prohibited past security checkpoints. Read the TSA’s Prohibited Items list for more information about prohibited items in carry-on and checked baggage.

SHUTTLE

SpeediShuttle provides affordable on demand door to door service from the HNL airport to your home, office or hotel. Departing within 20 minutes of check-in with Shuttle Attendant at pickup zone. Shuttle stops are conveniently located on the ground level adjacent to baggage Claim C (HNL Interisland Terminal), as well as, curbside at the Overseas Terminal (between Baggage Claim D & E), in front of the HNL Commuter Terminal and on the median curb outside Baggage Claim H.

CITY BUS

The City & County of Honolulu provides an island-wide bus transportation system that serves Honolulu International Airport. Bus Stops are located on the second level roadway along the center median. Look for the signs. Buses routes number 19, 20 and 31 serve HNL airport. Click here for HNL fare and service information.

TAXIS

Taxi Service is available on the center median fronting the terminal baggage claim areas. See the taxi dispatchers (yellow shirts with black lettering, and the wording “TAXI DISPATCHER” for service. Click here for more information on taxis at HNL.

HNL CAR RENTAL

Car rental shuttles stop in designated areas along the center median on the ground level outside of baggage claim areas.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for HNL are $15 for short-term domestic parking, $15 for short-term international parking and $12 for long-term Economy parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the HNL Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available at Lot M Garage located adjacent to the Interisland Terminal and Lot D located directly across from the Overseas Terminal. Click here for hourly parking rates.

INTERNATIONAL

International Parking is available in Lot A, adjacent to International Terminal.. Maximum rate of $15 for 24 hours. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking Lot B for $12 per 24 hours. Economy lots is located adjacent to the Commuter Terminal. Click here for hourly parking rates.

BICYCLE

HNL offers free courtesy parking for bicycles at four ground level locations: South of Baggage Claim Area A, International Parking Structure – in the vicinity of the elevators, Overseas Parking Structure – East end and Overseas Parking Structure – West end. Bicycle parking is restricted to a maximum 72 hours (three days).

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The HNL Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. The Ewa Cell Phone Waiting Area is located on Service Road A near the Airport Post Office (intersection of Nimitz Highway and Aolele Street). Entrance from Nimitz Highway.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all HNL parking facilities. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at HNL.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Designated parking spaces for electric vehicles are available in the HNL International Arrivals Parking structure at various locations on levels 1, 5, 6, 7 and 8. Parking rates apply. Click here for more information about EV parking at HNL.
