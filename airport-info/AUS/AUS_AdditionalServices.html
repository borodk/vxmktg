ANIMAL RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at AUS airport’s landscaped and lighted dog walk. Located outside the lower level at the far east and west ends of the parking garage.

HILTON AUSTIN AIRPORT HOTEL

The Hilton Austin Airport Hotel is is 0.7 miles to the AUS Austin-Bergstrom International Airport and 6.9 miles to downtown Austin. The hotel offers amenities such as a fitness center, coin laundry, gift shop, and conference and meeting rooms. Day membership to the Health Center is available. ART AT THE AIRPORT

The Austin Art at the Airport Program is featured at AUS in dozens of sculptures​, paintings, murals, and exhibits throughout the airport. Some works are permanent and fixed in their settings, while others change on occasion.
