DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for aus are $23 for short-term domestic parking and $17 for long-term Economy parking, and $18-27 for short-term Valet parking (24hr minimum stay required).

RESERVATIONS

None of the AUS Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available Garages 1 and 2 adjacent to Main Terminal. Click here for hourly parking rates.

INTERNATIONAL

International Parking is available in Lot A, adjacent to Main Terminal. Maximum rate of $11 for 24 hours. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking Lots E-G for $7 per 24 hours. Follow the Overhead Signs on Presidential Blvd. Reference Long-Term Lots. Free shuttle service runs continuously between On-Airport economy lots and the terminal.

VALET

Valet parking is available $18-27 daily for Garages 1 and 2. Click here for hourly parking rates.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The AUS Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located at 2801 Spirit of Texas drive, take the Airport Cargo/Service Entrance exit (note: Economy Parking Lot J and H are closed). Flight arrival information display available on location.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all AUS parking facilities. In the garage, these spots are located at the end nearest the terminal. In the surface lots, handicap parking is adjacent to the shuttle stops. Our shuttle buses are equipped with wheelchair lifts.
