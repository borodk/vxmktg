SWEET EXTRAS

AVIATION MUSEUM

Enjoy an entertaining and informative view of the history of aviation in Southern Nevada, 24 hours a day, seven days a week at the Howard W. Cannon Aviation Museum.

CAR WASHING SERVICES

Choose to have your car washed and detailed when receiving valet service at LAS.

KID'S PLAY AREA

Keep young travelers entertained before their flight with the LAS Kid's Play Area. Located on Level 2 of Terminal 1.

GAMING

Gamble before leaving or arriving at LAS at slot machines located throughout the airport. Must be 21 or older to play.

LAS MEDICAL CENTER

The LAS Medical Center provides medical services to travelers such as vaccinations, screening tests and other basic medical care. Most major insurances are accepted.

PET RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an LAS Pet Relief Area. Located in Terminal 3 outside of Baggage Claim.

SELECT PHYSICAL THERAPY

Receive physical therapy services before your flight from Select Physical Therapy in the Ticketing Area of Terminal 3.
