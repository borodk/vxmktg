AIRPORT SHUTTLE

LAS provides shuttle service every 12-15 minutes between Terminal 1, Terminal 3, and McCarran Rent-A-Car Center. Shuttle stops are located on Level Zero of the Terminal 1 Parking Garage and outside Level Zero of Terminal 3.

SHUTTLE

Shuttle service between LAS and surrounding Las Vegas destinations is located on the west side of Terminal 1 Baggage Claim, outside doors 7-13. For Terminal 3 domestic travelers, service is located on the west side of Level Zero. For Terminal 3 international travelers, service is located on the east side of Level Zero. Select shuttle service providers offer service from LAS to surrounding hotels and resorts, and inter-city locations.

PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION

The Regional Transportation Commission of Southern Nevada (RTC) provides bus service to and from LAS Airport. Terminal 1 service is provided by Route 108, Route 109, and Westcliff Airport Express (WAX) route. Terminal 3 service is provided by WAX and Centennial Express (CX) routes. RTC bus stops are located on Level Zero of Terminal 1 and the west side of Level Zero of Terminal 3. Follow orange signs for Public Transportation. RTC Paratransit service is available at Terminals 1 and 3 by reservation to those eligible. Click here for more information about RTC services at LAS.

TAXIS

Taxis are located at Terminal 1 on the east side of Baggage Claim, outside doors 1- 4, and outside Level Zero of Terminal 3. Taxis are also available at McCarran Rent-A-Car Center. There is a $2.00 fee for taxis originating at LAS. Click here to view all taxi service providers at LAS.

LAS CAR RENTAL

LAS provides shuttle service from Terminal 3 to McCarran Rent-A-Car Center every 5 minutes at Ground Transportation outside Baggage Claim. Metered parking is available for 30 minutes at the Center.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for LAS are $36 for short-term parking, $16 for long-term parking, and $10 for Economy parking.

RESERVATIONS

LAS Parking Facilities do not accept parking reservations.

SHORT-TERM

Short-term parking is available at parking garages adjacent to Terminal 1 on Level 2M and adjacent to Terminal 3 on Level 1 for $2 per hour, $3 per hour after 3 hours, and $36 maximum daily . Short-term parking is available for free for 15 minutes to those awaiting or dropping off travelers. For domestic parking at Terminal 3, use the west side of the garage. For international parking at Terminal 3, use the east side of the garage.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available at parking garages adjacent to Terminal 1 on Levels 1M, 3, 4, 5, 6 and adjacent to Terminal 3 on Levels 2-6 for $2 the first 30 minutes, $3 the first hour, and $16 maximum daily. 30 day-maximum parking limit.

ECONOMY

Economy parking is available free the first 15 minutes, $2 the first 30 minutes, $3 per 30 minutes - 2 hours, $1 per hour after 2 hours, and $10 maximum daily. Terminal 1 Economy Parking is located at 576 Kitty Hawk Way, off of Paradise Rd. Terminal 3 Economy Parking is located adjacent to Terminal 3 Parking Garage.

VALET

Valet parking at LAS is provided by Ampco Airpark. Service is available on Level 2 of Terminal 1 Parking Garage and Level 5 of Terminal 3 Parking Garage for $6 minimum, $1 per additional hour, and $23 maximum daily. Car wash and detail services offered. Call 702.261.6999 for more information.

MOTORCYCLE

Designated motorcycle parking is available in the center of Level 2 of the Long-Term Parking Garage. Use cashier booths when exiting to ensure safety and pay for parking fees.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

LAS does not have a Cell Phone Waiting Lot. However, there is no charge for the first 15 minutes of parking in Short-Term Terminal Garages. To avoid a charge, insert parking ticket at exit gates. Do not use Pay Stations.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all LAS parking facilities.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle parking is currently not available at LAS.

EXPRESS EXIT

Expedite the parking process with Express Exit. Take an Express Exit ticket when entering a parking facility. Use this ticket to pay for parking fees at Pay Stations located throughout LAS. When leaving the parking facility, use your Express Exit ticket in designated Express Exit lanes to leave parking facilities faster.
