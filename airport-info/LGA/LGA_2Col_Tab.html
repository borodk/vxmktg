TRAFFIC

To avoid traffic at LGA, sign up for Port Authority Alerts.

AIRPORT SHUTTLE

LGA provides shuttle service to all terminals and parking facilities. Route A buses provide service to all terminals every 15 minutes 6:00 A.M. - 10:30 P.M. Route B buses provide service to Central Terminal Building (Terminal B), Terminal C and Terminal D every 5 minutes 10:00 A.M. - 8:00 P.M.

NYC AIRPORTER

Use NYC Airporter for bus service between LGA to Manhattan. Buses arrive and depart every 30 minutes from Port Authority Bus Terminal, Grand Central Station, and Penn Station. Tickets are $13. Purchase tickets online at www.NYCAirporter.com.

NYC BUS SERVICE

The MTA New York City Transit offers bus service between LGA to Manhattan and Queens with connections to further destinations through New York City subway service. For bus service to Manhattan, use bus M60. For bus service to western Queens or midtown Manhattan, use bus Q70 limited or Q47. For service to central Queens, use buses Q72 or Q48. Click here for MTA connection information.

TAXIS

Taxi service can be found at taxi stands outside of all terminals. Ground Transportation Information Counters located on the Lower Level of Baggage Claim provide information about reservations for buses, shared vans and private cars. Click here for more information about taxi service at LGA.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for LGA are $39 for short-term parking lots, $39 for Economy Parking Lot P3, and $6 per additional 8 hour period after the first 48 hours at Economy Parking Lot P3.

RESERVATIONS

Reservations are available for short-term parking facilities Lot P2 and P4 for $5. Make your reservation online when you book your flight.

SHORT-TERM PARKING

Short-term parking is available at Lot P6 and P7 for Terminal A, Lot P1 and P2 for Terminal B, Lot P4 for Terminal C, and Lot P5 for Terminal D at $4 per half hour, $8 per hour, and $39 daily maximum.

LONG-TERM PARKING

Long-term parking is available at Economy Parking Lot P3 located between Terminals B and C for $39 the first 24 hours, $78 for 48 hours, and $6 per 8 hour period after 48 hours. LGA provides shuttle service from all terminals to Economy Parking Lot P3 on Route A and Route B buses.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

There is no Cell Phone Waiting Lot available at LGA.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking spaces are designated in short-term parking lots near terminal entrance.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Five electric vehicle charging stations are located on the Ground Level of Terminal B at Parking Lot P2. Charging station access is free with an RFID enabled credit card or Chargepoint access card. Electricity is provided free of charge by Chargepoint. Parking rates apply.

MOTORCYCLE

Free motorcycle parking is available at designated parking spaces in Parking Lot P7 of Terminal A. LGA Route A shuttle buses provide service between Parking Lot P7 and all terminals.

E-ZPASS PLUS

Travelers with E-ZPass Plus accounts can apply LGA parking fees to their account by utilizing E-ZPass Plus available exit lanes at LGA Parking Facilities. Click here for more information use E-ZPass to pay for parking fees at LGA.
