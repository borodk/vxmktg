Terminal 2 to check in for all departing flights. (Note: arrival flights from Mexico arrive in the International Terminal, Concourses A and G.)

5:15 A.M. – 11:30 P.M. Pacific Time

CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

5:00 A.M - 8:00 P.M. Pacific Time

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

ARRIVAL

For all domestic arrivals, Virgin America uses baggage claim carousels 3 and 4 in Terminal 2. All international arrivals use Carousel 9 in the International Terminal.

TSA PRE CHECK

TSA Pre✓™ expedited screening is available at Terminal 2 of SFO 4:30 A.M. - 12:30 A.M.

PROHIBITED ITEMS

Certain sharp objects, liquids, foods, and gifts are not prohibited past security checkpoints. Read the TSA's Prohibited Items list for more information about prohibited items in carry-on and checked baggage.

CLEAR

SFO post-security connectors are located between Terminal 1 (Gates 40-48) and Terminal 2 (Gates 50-59), and International Terminal (Gates G91-G102) and Terminal 3 (Gates 60-90).

ADDRESS

BUSIEST TRAVEL TIMES

For pickups and drop offs during busy travel times, use the arrival level during the day and the departure level at night to avoid traffic.

TRAFFIC

Stay in left-hand lanes to avoid Terminal 1 traffic when driving to the Virgin America ticket counter at Terminal 2 of SFO.

LONG-TERM PARKING SHUTTLE

SFO provides complimentary shuttle service 24 hours a day from SFO’s Long-Term Parking Facility. Shuttle departures are every 5 minutes 4:15 A.M. - 12:15 A.M. and every 15 minutes 12:15 A.M. - 4:15 A.M. Electronic screens in SFO Parking Facilities display bus arrivals.

AIRTRAIN TO TERMINALS

Operating 24 hours a day, year-long, SFO’s AirTrain provides service to all terminals, terminal garages, and the BART Station via Red Line or Blue Line. Service to the Rental Car Center is provided by the AirTrain Blue Line. AirTrain does not provide service to SFO’s long-term parking facilities. Station departures are as frequent as every four minutes.

BART RAPID RAIL

BART offers services around the Bay Area direct to SFO. Use SFO’s AirTrain to access the BART Station from all terminals, and terminal garages via the Garage G/BART Station stop located at the Departure/Ticketing Level of the International Terminal. Click here for more information about SFO’s public transit.

CALTRAIN COMMUTER RAIL

Use the BART Millbrae station to connect between SFO and Caltrain. Click here for more information about SFO’s public transit.

SAMTRANS PUBLIC SERVICE

SamTrans 24-hour bus service connects SFO to the San Mateo County and parts of San Francisco. Buses stop at the Arrivals/Baggage Claim Level of Terminal 2, and Level 1 of the International Terminal. Click here for more information about SFO’s public transit.

TAXIS

There is a taxi stand available outside the arrivals level. Click here for more information on SFO Taxis.

UBER AND LYFT

Find airport service information at Uber San Francisco or Lyft San Francisco.

CAR SHARING

Use point-to-point car sharing services to get around town for the day. Zipcar is available from the Rental Car Center Avis lot at SFO, or hop on the shuttle to Skypark to access DriveNow all-electric BMWs. You will need to create online accounts to access the vehicles.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for SFO are $18 for long-term parking, $28 for international parking, and $36 for domestic parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the SFO Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC SHORT-TERM

Domestic Terminal parking is available for $2 per 20 minutes and $36 maximum for 24 hours.

INTERNATIONAL SHORT-TERM

International Terminal parking is available for $2 per 20 minutes and $28 maximum for 24 hours.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available for $2 per 20 minutes and $18 maximum for 24 hours.

PARK FAST

Park Fast allows travelers to bypass entry and exit lines at SFO Parking Facilities and enjoy reserved covered parking on the fourth floor of Domestic Terminal parking. Apply now and save $2 a day.

PARK VALET

Park Valet at SFO Domestic Terminal parking provides 24-hour valet service with complimentary Wall Street Journal and charging stations for electric vehicles. Park Valet is available on the fourth floor of Domestic Terminal parking for $38 the first 24 hours and $48 every additional 24 hours.

BICYCLE

SFO provides courtesy bicycle racks for parking at Domestic Terminal Parking Garage Level 4, International Terminal Parking Garage Level 1, SFO Rental Car Center, and Westfield Parking Garage. Bicycles can be parked up to 14 days. Bicycles parked for more than 24 hours must be registered. Learn more about SFO’s bicycle parking here.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The SFO Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights for up to one hour. Located at 806 South Airport Boulevard San Francisco, CA 94128. Vehicles must be attended at all times. Flight arrival information kiosk and vending machine on location. The Lot is closed 1:00 A.M. - 5:00 A.M.

KISS & FLY

Avoid SFO terminal congestion by dropping off loved ones at Kiss & Fly. Located at the SFO Rental Car Center on North McDonnell Road. SFO AirTrain Blue Line provides service from Kiss & Fly to all terminals.

ACCESSIBILITY

Accessible parking is available in all SFO Parking Facilities. In Domestic and International Terminal Parking Garages, accessible parking spaces are adjacent to elevator lobbies. In Long-Term Parking, accessible parking spaces are located on the first floor. Additionally, SFO’s Long-Term Parking shuttle buses are wheelchair accessible. Learn more about accessible parking at SFO.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle charging stations are located in SFO International Terminal Parking Garage A, International Terminal Parking Garage G, Long-Term and Domestic Parking Garages, and Park Valet. Some stations require a connection cable. Find stations here.

FASTRAK

Travelers with FasTrak accounts can apply SFO parking fees to their account by utilizing FasTrak available entry and exit lanes at SFO Parking Facilities.
