SWEET EXTRAS

ANIMAL RELIEF AREAS

Traveling can be ruff! Find relief for your pet at an SFO Animal Relief Area, 24 hours a day. Located in Terminal 2, Courtyard 3 of the Arrivals/Baggage Claim Level. Click here for more information about traveling with pets at SFO.

CAR WASHING SERVICES

Return to a clean car in any SFO Parking Facility when you reserve a car wash by at least 3:00 p.m. the day before your return flight. To make a reservation, please visit www.buymywash.com.

SHOWER FACILITIES

Freshen up before your flight with SFO’s available-to-rent shower facilities. Located in the SFO International Terminal by Boarding Area G, pre-security. Toiletries offered.

KIDS’ SPOT

Keep young travelers entertained before their flight with SFO’s interactive play area, Kids’ Spot. Located in Terminal 2 of SFO.

SELF-GUIDED TERMINAL TOUR

Spend time exploring your terminal before going through security with SFO’s self-guided tour. Prizes are available from 8 A.M. - 8 P.M.

SFO MEDICAL CLINIC

Receive medical services before your international flight from the SFO Medical Clinic, located in the International Terminal Main Hall. Open Monday-Friday, 7:00 A.M. - 7:00 P.M. and Saturday, 9:00 A.M. - 4:00 P.M. Click here for more information.

SFO MUSEUM EXHIBITS

Learn something new before or after security as you walk to your gate. Map of SFO exhibitions.

YOGA ROOM

Find your zen, post-security, before boarding your flight. Open daily in Terminal 2 of SFO.
