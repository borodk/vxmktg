SHUTTLE

Shuttle service at SEA-TAC is located at Door 00 on the Baggage Claim Level. Click here to view shuttle companies serving SEA-TAC.

LINK LIGHT RAIL

Link Light Rail offers train service between SEA-TAC and downtown Seattle every 7.5-15 minutes Monday - Saturday 5:00 A.M. - 1:00 A.M. and Sunday 6:00 A.M. - 12:00 A.M. Located on the fourth floor of the parking garage. Click here for schedule information.

KING COUNTY METRO TRANSIT

King County Metro offers bus service between SEA-TAC and surrounding Seattle locations. Service to SEA-TAC is located on International Blvd and S 176th St near Link Light Rail Station. Use the pedestrian bridge on the fourth floor of the SEA-TAC parking garage to access the Link Light Rail Station and exit International Blvd. Transit information is provided at SEA-TAC Ground Transportation Information Booth by Door 16 of the Baggage Claim Level.

SOUND TRANSIT

Sound Transit Express offers bus service between SEA-TAC and surrounding Seattle locations on bus routes 560 and 574. Route 560 provides service from West Seattle to Bellevue, and Route 574 provides service from Lakewood to SEA-TAC. Located at the Main Terminal bus stop on the Lower Level.

TAXIS

Taxi cab service is provided by Seattle Yellow Cab. Taxi stands are located on the third floor of SEA-TAC parking garage. For service, call 206.622.6500.

LIMOUSINES

Limousine service to or from SEA-TAC is provided by STILA. To request service, call 206.243.1811 or use kiosks at Travelers’ Information Boards in Baggage Claim or the Ground Transportation Booth on the third floor of the parking garage.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for SEA-TAC are $35 for Terminal Direct parking, and $28 for General Parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the SEA-TAC Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

SHORT-TERM

Short-term hourly parking is available in Terminal Direct parking on the fourth floor of SEA-TAC Airport Parking Garage for $4 per hour. Additional short-term hourly parking is available in General Parking on Floors 1-3 and 5-8 of SEA-TAC Airport Parking Garage for $3 per hour. Main Terminal access is available via skybridges located on the fourth floor of the garage.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Terminal Direct parking on the fourth floor of SEA-TAC Airport Parking Garage for $35 per 24 hours and in General Parking on Floors 1-3 and 5-8 for $28 per 24 hours or $130 per week.

BICYCLE

Bicycle parking is available at SEA-TAC on the fourth floor of the parking garage by Skybridges 1 and 6, and on the Arrivals Level at the south-end Bus Stop. Long-term storage is available on the Baggage Claim Level by Carousels 12 and 13.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The SEA-TAC Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available for free to those awaiting arrival flights up to 20 minutes. Located across from the S 170th St exit of Airport Expressway.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all SEA-TAC parking facilities. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at SEA-TAC.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

48 electric vehicle parking spaces are available at SEA-TAC on Level 4 and 5 of the Main Parking Garage. Parking rates apply.

MOTORCYCLE

Motorcycle parking is available in SEA-TAC parking garage. Parking rates for General Parking and Terminal Direct apply.

PREMIER CORPORATE PARKING

Premier Corporate Parking is a SEA-TAC parking membership available to select companies. Members use their Premier access card and personal credit card to track and pay for parking fees. The parking rate is $20 per 24 hours or portion thereof. Premier parking is available on Level 1-3, and 5-8 of the main parking garage. Request an application at premierparking@portseattle.org.

EASY PAY

Pay for parking before you exit the garage with Easy Pay machines located on the fourth floor of the parking garage. When leaving the parking garage, use your Easy Pay receipt at any exit lane to leave the parking garage faster.
