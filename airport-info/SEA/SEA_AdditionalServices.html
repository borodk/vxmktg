SWEET EXTRAS

ANIMAL RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet an SEA-TAC Animal Relief Area. Located outside the Main Terminal on the north and south ends of the Baggage Claim Level and inside Concourse B of Main Terminal across from Hudson News.

BICYCLE ASSEMBLY STATION

The Bicycle Assembly Station is available to those who need to assemble or disassemble their bicycle before or after traveling. Located across from Ken’s Baggage & Frozen Foods on the Baggage Claim Level between Carousels 12 and 13.

CONFERENCE CENTER

Work on the fly. Reserve a conference room at The Conference Center at SEA-TAC Monday - Friday 7:00 A.M. - 5:00 P.M. Located pre security on the Mezzanine Level above Security Checkpoint 1.

KID’S PLAY ROOM

Keep young travelers entertained before their flight with SEA-TAC’s interactive play area, Kid’s Play Room. Located post security between Central Terminal and Terminal A.

MOTHER’S ROOM

Located inside the Kid's Play Room, the Mother’s Room is a quiet, private space for moms to nurse or relax with their babies before their flight with adjustable lighting, rocking chairs, foot stools, and electrical outlets.

MUSIC PROGRAM

Experience the City of Music Program promotes the Northwest music culture at SEA-TAC with live performances, overhead music, web radio, art exhibits, and video clips throughout the airport to enhance the traveler’s experience. Click here to view the live music schedule, or submit your music to the program.

PAY-PER-USE PHONE

SEA-TAC offers pay-per-use internet enabled phones for 35 cents a minute and $5 minimum. Located across Seattle’s Best Coffee pre security and post security at Gate B7.
