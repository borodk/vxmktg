ADDITIONAL SERVICES

PET RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an EWR Pet Relief Area, 24 hours a day. Located outside terminal arrivals area. Click here for more information about traveling with pets at EWR.

EWR MEDICAL OFFICE

Receive medical services before or after your flight from the EWR Medical Office. Walk-ins are available Monday - Friday 8:30 A.M. - 5:30 P.M. Drug and alcohol testing is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Bus service to the EWR Medical Office is provided from all terminals. Click here for directions.
