AIRTRAIN

Operating 24 hours a day, year-long, EWR’s AirTrain provides free transportation to all terminals, parking garages, rental car facilities, and hotel shuttles and reservations. Connection between EWR and New Jersey Transit, PATH and AmTrak is available for $5.50 through the Newark International Airport Station. The EWR AirTrain arrives/departs every 3 minutes 5:00 A.M. - 12:00 A.M., every 15 minutes 12:00 A.M. - 5:00 A.M., and Sundays every 15 minutes 12:00 A.M. - 7:00 A.M., taking two minutes between terminals. For more information, view the EWR AirTrain Map.

NEW JERSEY TRANSIT

NJ Transit offers rail service to and from EWR on the Northeast Corridor and North Jersey Coast lines. At least six trains arrive at the Newark International Airport Station every hour weekdays 6:00 A.M. - 9:00 P.M. and at least four trains every hour 9:00 P.M. - 12:00 A.M. NJ Transit does not offer rail service 2 A.M. - 5 A.M. Click here for schedule and fare information.

PATH

PATH provides rapid rail service between Manhattan and New Jersey. Use Newark Penn Station to connect to PATH.

AMTRAK

AmTrak provides hourly train service on the Northeast Corridor between Newark International Airport Station, New York City, and Philadelphia. Click here to learn where to purchase AmTrak tickets at EWR.

NEWARK AIRPORT EXPRESS BUS

Newark Airport Express Buses provide service between EWR and New York City for $16 one-way and $28 roundtrip. Stations are located at bus stop 5 of Terminal A, bus stop 2 of Terminal B, and bus stops 5 and 6 of Terminal C. Buses arrive every 15 minutes during the day and depart every 30 minutes before 6:45 A.M. and after 11:15 P.M. Click here for more information about bus service at EWR.

TRANS-BRIDGE LINES

Trans-Bridge Lines offers daily bus service westward between EWR and Pennsylvania.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for EWR Parking Facilities are $33 for short-term parking, $24 for Lot P1 and Lot P3 long-term parking, $27 for Lot P4 long-term parking, $18 for Economy parking, and $40 for Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

Parking reservations for EWR Parking Facilities are available online for designated areas in P3, P4, P6, Lot A, Lot B and Terminal C garages.

SHORT-TERM

PShort-term parking is available in Terminal Parking Lots A, B, and C for $4 the first 30 minutes, $8 the first hour, $4 every 30 minutes after the first hour, and $33 maximum for 24 hours.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Lot P1, Lot P3, and Lot P4 for $4 the first 30 minutes, $8 the first hour, and $4 every 30 minutes after first hour. Lot P1 and Lot P3 have a maximum rate of $24 for 24 hours. Lot P4 has a maximum rate of $27 for 24 hours. If you plan to park at EWR for more than 30 days, contact ABM at 973.961.6421 or 973.961.6422 the day of your arrival.

ECONOMY

Economy parking is available at Lot P6, (3 miles from the Terminal Area), for $18 the first 24-hours and $6 every 8-hour period after the first 24 hours. Shuttle service is provided from Lot P6 to Terminals A, B, and C. Shuttle departs every 10 minutes, 24-hours a day.

VALET

Covered valet parking is available next to Lot P4 for $40 the first 24-hours, and $20 every 12-hour period after. Reservations for Valet parking is not needed. AirTrain provides service from valet parking to all Terminals.

MOTORCYCLE

Motorcycle parking is available for free in Parking Lot P4, adjacent to Valet Parking. There are twenty covered, well-lit spots available.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The EWR Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available for free for those awaiting arrival flights for up to one hour. Entrance located on Basilone Road, 5 minutes south of airport terminals. Vehicles must be attended at all times. The Lot is open from 7 A.M to 1 A.M. daily.

ACCESSIBILITY

Accessible parking is available in EWR short-term Terminal Parking Garages A, B, and C, and long-term Parking Lot P4 for $4 the first 30 minutes, $8 the first hour, $4 every 30 minutes after the first hour, and $18 maximum for 24-hours.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle charging stations are located on the ground level of Parking Lot P4. Charging station access is free with an RFID enabled credit card or Chargepoint access card.

E-ZPASS PLUS

Travelers with E-ZPass Plus accounts can apply EWR parking fees to their account by utilizing E-ZPass Plus available exit lanes at EWR Parking Facilities.
