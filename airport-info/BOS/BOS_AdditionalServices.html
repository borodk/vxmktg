SWEET EXTRAS

ANIMAL RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at a BOS Animal Relief Area, 24 hours a day. Located outside the Terminal C Baggage Claim on the Arrivals Level.

ART PROGRAM

The Boston Logan Public Art Program features exhibitions, and artwork throughout the airport educating travelers about the City of Boston, and the region of New England.

KIDPORT

Keep young travelers entertained before their flight with the BOS play area, Kidport. Located in Terminal C post-security, in the area prior to Gate 25. Click here for more information about family friendly amenities at BOS.
