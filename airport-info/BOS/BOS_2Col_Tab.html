CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

Curbside baggage is currently not available at BOS.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

BUSIEST TRAVEL TIMES

The busiest travel time at BOS is 2:00 P.M. - 6:00 P.M.

TRAFFIC

To avoid traffic at BOS, use public transportation for service to the airport.

AIRPORT SHUTTLE

Massport provides bus shuttle service between all terminals and the Airport Station via the MBTA Blue Line. Shuttle buses run every 5 - 6 minutes. Use Shuttle Buses 11, 33 or 88 for service to Terminal C, subway station, and Rental Car Center. Click here for more information about the BOS bus shuttle service.

LOGAN EXPRESS

Logan Express provides nonstop bus service between BOS and surrounding locations; Braintree, Framingham, Woburn, and Peabody. Daily parking at Logan Express lots is $7. Travel time is approximately 30 - 45 minutes. Click here for more information.

MASSACHUSETTS BAY TRANSPORTATION AUTHORITY

Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA) offers transportation service between BOS Airport and Downtown Boston locations via MBTA Silver Line Bus Transit, Blue Line Subway and Commuter Boat services. Connections to MBTA Red Line and Commuter Rail are available via Silver Line, located at South Station. Connections to MBTA Green Line, Orange Line and Commuter Rail are available via Blue Line, located at North Station. MBTA ferry service to BOS is available at Fore River Shipyard, 703 Washington Street in Quincy, or from Pemberton Point in Hull. BOS airport shuttle bus, Massport Route 66, provides service from BOS ferry dock to all terminals. Click here for more information about MBTA services to BOS.

UBER AND LYFT

Find airport service information at Uber Boston and Lyft Boston. For pick up location, follow signs on the Arrivals Level for limousines/car service.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for BOS are $29 for short-term Terminal parking, and $20 for long-term economy parking. When parking is in high demand, attendants assist in parking vehicles.

RESERVATIONS

Reserved parking is available through the Lexus Parking Passport Gold program in Central Parking Garage and Terminal B Garage.

DOMESTIC

Parking for all domestic terminals is available in Central Parking, Terminal B and Terminal E Garages. Use pedestrian bridges on Level 4 of the Central Garage for access to all terminals. Click here for Terminal parking rates.

INTERNATIONAL

International parking is available in Terminal E Parking Lots for $29 the first 24 hours, $16 0-6 additional hours, and $29 per additional day. Located adjacent to International Terminal E. For covered parking, use Central Parking or Terminal B Garages. Click here for International Terminal E hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking for $20 the first 24 hours, $10 0-6 additional hours, $20 per additional day, and $120 per 7 days. Located at the intersection of Service Rd and Prescott St. Airport shuttle buses provide service from Economy Parking to all terminals every 15 - 20 minutes. Click here for Economy Parking hourly rates.

BICYCLE

Outdoor bicycle racks are available at Terminal A, Terminal E, Economy Parking Garage, Rental Car Center, Blue Line Airport Station, Bremen Street Park and Greenway Connector.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The BOS Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to those awaiting arrival flights for up to 30 minutes. Located at the intersection of Hotel Drive and Service Road, 5 minutes away from all airport terminals.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available in all parking garages at BOS, even when garage signs say “Full.” Click here for more information about accessibility services provided at BOS.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

26 electric vehicle charging stations are available at BOS located on Level 6 of Central Garage East, Level 6 of Central Garage Southeast, Level 3 of Terminal B Garage, on Level 6 of West Garage Southwest and Northwest. Parking rates apply. Click here for more information about EV, hybrid, and alternative fuel parking at BOS.

EXIT EXPRESS

Travelers with an Exit Express card can apply BOS parking fees to their account by using their Parking card at entry and exit parking garage readers or Pay Stations. Click here to enroll for Exit Express.

LEXUS PARKING PASSPORT GOLD

The Lexus Parking Passport Gold program is an annual membership that provides reserved parking spaces at BOS on Level 3 of the Central Parking Garage and Level 1 of Terminal B Garage. Apply parking fees to your Lexus Parking Card when entering and exiting BOS parking garages. Click here to enroll for Lexus Parking Passport Gold.
