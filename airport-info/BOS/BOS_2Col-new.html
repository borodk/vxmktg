Airport Information

WIFI

BOS provides complimentary WiFi in all terminals. To access, simply connect to the “loganwifi” network, or visit www.loganwifi.com.

CHARGING STATIONS

Charge your devices before you fly at charging stations built into BOS seating areas throughout the airport.

9/11 MEMORIAL

The Airport 9/11 Memorial at BOS honors the families affected by and the passengers and crews of American Airlines Flight 11 and United Airlines Flight 175 involved in the events of September 11, 2001.
