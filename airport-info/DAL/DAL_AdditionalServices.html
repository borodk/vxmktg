SWEET EXTRAS

ART PROGRAM

The Love Field Public Art Program features performances, exhibitions, and pieces of artwork throughout the airport representing the history and legacy of Dallas Love Field. Art can be viewed before and after security checkpoints. Click here for more information.

LIL LUV’ LOUNGE

Keep young travelers entertained before their flight with DAL’s entertainment area, Lil Luv’ Lounge. Located next to Creative Kids Stuff in the Terminal building. Click here for more information about family friendly amenities at DAL.

MUSEUM OF FLIGHT

Learn about the history and progress of aviation before your flight at the Frontier of Flight Museum, located on the southeast side of DAL. Open Monday - Saturday 10 A.M. - 5 P.M. and Sunday 1 P.M. - 5 P.M. Click here for direction and ticket information.

PET PARK

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at a DAL Pet Park. Located on the Upper Level of the Baggage Claim Hall or Ground Transportation Level, across from the taxi stand. Click here to learn more about travelling with pets at DAL.
