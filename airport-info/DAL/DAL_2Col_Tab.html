SHUTTLE

Multiple shuttle services are available to & from DAL including City Shuttle, Super Shuttle, and Yellow Checker Shuttle. Click here for more information about DAL Airport Shuttle Services.

DALLAS AREA RAPID TRANSIT

DART Rail provides public transportation for Addison, Carrollton, Cockrell Hill, Dallas, Farmers Branch, Garland, Glenn Heights, Highland Park, Irving, Richardson, Rowlett, Plano, and University Park. DART’s bus service, Love Link 524, provides free transportation between DAL and Inwood/Love Field Station to connecting North Texas destinations. DART Rail operates 5:00 A.M. - 12:00 A.M.

DART AIRPORT BUS SERVICE

DART’s Love Link 524 bus service provides free transportation between DAL and the Inwood/Love Field Station to surrounding North Texas destinations via DART Rail Green Line and Orange Line. Love Link 524 operates every 15 minutes before 9:30 A.M. and 4:00 - 6:00 P.M. Midday and evening departures are every 20 minutes, and every 30 minutes after 10:30 P.M. On weekends, the bus operates every 20 minutes 9:30 A.M. - 6:30 P.M. and every 30 minutes all other times. The DART Bus stop at Dallas Love Field is located on the Lower Level of the Terminal Building by Ground Transportation signs.

DALLAS YELLOW CAB

Dallas Yellow Cab provides taxi service to and from DAL. One-way fare between DAL and Dallas Central Business District (Downtown Dallas) is approximately $19. There is a $1 Love Field Loading Fee. Fare prices vary. Taxi Cab service is provided on the Upper Level of the Terminal Building across from Baggage Claim.

TAXIS

Taxi Cab service is provided on the upper level across the street from the baggage claim wing of the main terminal building. Click here for more information about taxi service at DAL.

UBER AND LYFT

Uber pickup is available on the lower Ground Level of the Terminal Building or check out Lyft Dallas airport service information.

CAR RENTAL

Car rental agencies are located on the arrivals level near baggage claim, look for the Rent-A-Car desk. After securing your DAL car rental, proceed outside the main entrance of the airport to the nearby transportation island. A rental courtesy shuttle bus will meet you there to take you to the Dallas airport car rentals facility.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for DAL are $17 for short-term parking, $13 for long-term parking, and $24 for valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

The Parking Spot (reservations accepted)
6900 Cedar Springs Road, Dallas, TX 75235

Parking Garage A (Short-Term) and Garage B (Long-Term) do not currently accept reservations.

SHORT-TERM

Short-term parking is available in Parking Garage A for free the first 30 minutes, $4 for 1 hour, $6 for 2 hours, $10 for 3 hours, $13 for 5 hours, and $17 maximum for 24 hours.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Parking Garage B for free the first 30 minutes, $3 for 1 hour, $5 for 2 hours, $7 for 3 hours, $10 for 5 hours, and $13 maximum for 24 hours.

VALET

Valet parking is available in Parking Garage A and B for $8 up to 1 hour, $13 up to 2 hours, $16 up to 3 hours, $19 3-5 hours, and $24 maximum for 24 hours. Click here or call 469.484.7722 for more information about valet parking at DAL.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The DAL Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available for free for those awaiting arriving flights. Located on the east side of the Cedar Springs Road, past the parking shuttle and Hertz Rental Car Facility. Vehicles must be attended at all times.

OFF-SITE

Off-site parking is available for DAL by BEST Parking, The Parking Spot, and The Parking Spot 2. Reservations available online.

ACCESSIBILITY

Passenger Pick-up and Drop-off Carts, Wheelchairs/Medical Transport Carts are provided by all airlines. Airline passengers should notify their travel agent, ticketing agent, flight attendant or gate agent when carts, wheelchairs or medical transportation is needed. There are two sets of elevators located between the main lobby and baggage claim. All restrooms at Love Field are ADA accessible. One children's/unisex restroom is located on the West Concourse across from Gate 2. Hearing impaired telephones (TTY) are located on the secured side of the airport in the suite next to the Fair Park Store and in Baggage Claim. These machines are located on pay phones.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle charging stations are located at DAL on Level 1 and Level 3 of Parking Garage A, and Level 1 of Parking Garage B.

TOLLTAG

Travelers with TollTag accounts can apply DAL parking fees to their account by utilizing TollTag available entry and exit lanes at DAL Parking Facilities. Click here for more information about using TollTag at DAL.
