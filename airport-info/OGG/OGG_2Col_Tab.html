CHECK-IN

Main Terminal, at kiosks 42-47.

COUNTER HOURS

Counter will open 3 hours prior to flight time and close 30 minutes prior to flight time.

CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

Curbside check-in is not available at OGG.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

ARRIVAL

Baggage will be available for pick up from either carousels 1 or 2.

BAGGAGE SERVICE HOURS

There will be a Teammate up until 30 minutes after flight arrival offering baggage assistance.

TSA PRE CHECK

TSA Pre✓™ expedited security screening is available in OGG in the main terminal from 10am-2pm.

SHUTTLE

OGG Airport Shuttle Service counter is located in the Baggage Claim area. No reservations are needed. Offering one way and roundtrip services between Kahului Airport and Maui's lodging properties throughout Honokawai, Ka'anapali, Kahana, Kahului, Kihei, Lahaina, Makena, Napili and Wailea.

CITY BUS

The County of Maui operates a public transit system that provides limited service to OGG airport. The Maui Bus stops at marked locations fronting the terminal. The routes are operated daily.Buses routes number 40 and 35 serve OGG airport. Click here for OGG fare and service information.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for OGG are $10 for all parking garages. Click here for hourly parking rates.

RESERVATIONS

None of the OGG Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The OGG Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located curbside along Hemaloa Street, next to the UPS cargo facilities, just a few minutes away from the airport terminal.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all OGG parking facilities. Accessible spaces are generally located near crosswalks. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at OGG.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

The main public parking lot also includes an electric vehicle charging station which can provide service to two vehicles. Parking rates apply. Click here for more information about EV parking at OGG.
