SHUTTLE

DCA provides free shuttle service to all terminals, Metro Trail and Parking Garages. Shuttles buses operate every eight to ten minutes.

METRO TRAIL SYSTEMS

The Washington, DC Metrorail system has an elevated Metrorail station connected to the concourse level of terminals B and C at DCA Airport. Metrorail farecards may be purchased at machines located at all entrances to the Airport Metrorail station.

AMTRAK

DCA National Airport is located near 2 Amtrak rail stations: Amtrak Union Station and Amtrak Alexandria Station. Both are easily accessed using the Metrorail system.

GREYHOUND BUS

DCA National Airport is located near 2 Greyhound rail stations: Greyhound Washington, D.C. terminal and Greyhound Springfield, VA terminal. Both are easily accessed using the Metrorail system.

TAXIS

Taxi Stands are located on the arrivals curb just outside the baggage claim areas of each terminal. Uniformed taxicab dispatchers are stationed at each taxicab dispatch area to assist passengers. Click here for more information about Taxis at DCA.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for DCA are $25-36 for short-term and $15 for long-term Economy parking.

RESERVATIONS

Use e-Park to make an reservation for DCA Parking Lots.

SHORT-TERM

Short-term hourly parking is available at Short-Term Lot. Daily parking is available at Terminal Parking Garages A, B, and C for $25 maximum. Short-Term Lot is not intended for extended overnight parking. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking Lot for $15 per 24 hours, there is no hourly parking rate.

BICYCLE

Trail makes bicycling to the airport a convenient alternative. DCA airport has self-service bicycle storage racks outdoors which are available at no charge on a first-come, first-served basis. Click here for more information.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The DCA Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights for one hour. Located near the end of the “Return to Airport” ramp just beyond Terminal B/C.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all DCA parking facilities. All airport shuttle buses are fully accessible. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at DCA.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Four electric vehicle charging stations are available at DCA on Ground Level of Daily Garage B. Parking rates apply. Click here for more information about EV parking at DCA.
