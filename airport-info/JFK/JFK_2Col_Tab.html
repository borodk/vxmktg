LONG-TERM PARKING SHUTTLE

JFK provides complimentary shuttle service 24 hours a day from JFK’s Long-Term Parking Facility to AirTrain.

AIRTRAIN

Operating 24 hours a day, year-long, JFK’s AirTrain provides service to all terminals, parking facilities, Federal Circle Station, and Lefferts Boulevard via Jamaica Station and Howard Station lines. The All Terminals Train arrives every 10 minutes, and takes two minutes between terminals. All rental car facilities, hotel shuttles and reservations, and train transfer stations are located at Federal Circle Station. Use Jamaica Station and Howard Station lines for service to Federal Circle Station. Kiss & Fly and long-term parking Lot 9 are located at Lefferts Boulevard. For service to Lefferts Boulevard, use the Howard Station Line. To connect to the MTA Long Island Rail Road (LIRR) and New York City Transit, use Jamaica and Howard Beach stations via Jamaica Station and Howard Station lines. There is a $5 fee to enter or exit JFK at Jamaica and Howard Beach stations. AirTrain service within JFK is free.

NYC TRANSIT SUBWAY

The MTA New York City Transit offers subway services between Manhattan and JFK. Use JFK’s AirTrain Jamaica Station and Howard Station lines to access the NYC Transit Subway from Jamaica or Howard Beach stations. Click here for more information about JFK’s public transit.

LONG ISLAND RAIL ROAD

Use JFK’s Jamaica Station Line to connect with MTA’s LIRR by AirTrain. Click here for more information about JFK’s public transit.

NYC AIRPORTER

Use NYC Airporter for bus service between JFK to Manhattan. Buses arrive and depart every 30 minutes from Port Authority Bus Terminal, Grand Central Station, and Penn Station. Tickets are $16 one-way, and $30 roundtrip. Purchase tickets online at www.NYCAirporter.com.

TAXIS

There is a taxi stand available on the arrivals level. Click here for more information about taxi service at JFK.

UBER AND LYFT

Find airport service information at Uber New York or Lyft New York City.

BUS SERVICE

Use JFK’s AirTrain to Terminal 5 to connect to buses Q10, Q3 and B15 for service to Queens and East New York. Click here for schedules and more information about MTA’s bus service.

TRANS-BRIDGE LINES

Trans-Bridge Lines provides service daily 3:30 P.M. and 7:00 P.M. from the JFK Terminal 4 bus stop. Trans-Bridge Lines does not offer service between JFK and New York City. One-way tickets are available and cannot be purchased online. Click here for more information about service to and from JFK.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for JFK’s long-term and disability parking is $18 the first 24 hours, $6 each additional 8-hour period for long-term parking, and $33 for short-term parking.

RESERVATIONS

Reservations are available for short-term parking facilities for $5. Make your reservation online when you book your flight.

SHORT-TERM

Short-term parking is available for Terminals 1, 2, 4, 5, 7, 9 at the Green, Blue, Yellow, Orange and Red Parking Garages for $4 the first 30 minutes, $8 the first hour, $4 every 30 minutes after the first hour, and $33 maximum for 24 hours.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available at Lot 9, (located at Lefferts Boulevard, 4 miles from the Terminal Area), for $18 the first 24 hours, and $6 every 8-hour period after the first 24 hours.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The JFK Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available for free for those awaiting arrival flights for up to one hour. Located near the JFK airport entrance off the Van Wyck Expressway, less than 5 minutes away from airport terminals. Vehicles must be attended at all times. Open daily.

KISS & FLY

Avoid JFK terminal congestion by dropping off loved ones at Kiss & Fly. Located at JFK’s AirTrain Lefferts Boulevard Station. JFK AirTrain provides service from Kiss & Fly to all terminals.

ACCESSIBILITY

Accessible parking is available at JFK for $4 the first 30 minutes, $8 the first hour, $4 every 30 minutes after the first hour, and $18 maximum for 24-hours.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Five electric vehicle charging stations are located on the ground level of the Yellow Parking Garage, adjacent to Terminal 5. Charging station access is free with an RFID enabled credit card or Chargepoint access card.

E-ZPASS PLUS

Travelers with E-ZPass Plus accounts can apply JFK parking fees to their account by utilizing E-ZPass Plus available exit lanes at JFK Parking Facilities.

MOTORCYCLE

Motorcycle parking is available for free at JFK. Head toward signs for long-term parking and “Kiss and Fly.” There are twenty covered, well-lit spots available.
