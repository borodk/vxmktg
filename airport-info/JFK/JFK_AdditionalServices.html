AIRPORT PLAZA

Fuel up and fill up with Sunoco Diesel & Gas and a food court at the JFK Airport Plaza. Other services offered include, electric vehicle charging, dry cleaning, car wash, auto repair, free Wi-Fi, and more. Flight Information Display Boards on location. Click here for more information.

PET RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an JFK Pet Relief Area, 24 hours a day. Located outside terminal arrivals area. Click here for more information about traveling with pets at JFK.

JFK MEDICAL OFFICE

Receive medical services before or after your flight from the JFK Medical Office. Walk-ins are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Appointments are scheduled during normal business hours, Monday through Friday from 8 A.M. - 7 P.M. or Saturday from 10 A.M. - 3 P.M. Shuttle service to the JFK Medical Office is provided from all terminals. Click here for directions.
