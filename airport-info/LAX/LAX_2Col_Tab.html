BUSIEST ARRIVAL TIMES

Sunday nights are the busiest arrival times. During busy arrival times, pick up passengers at the Upper/Departures level. They can take the escalator or elevator up to departures.

BUSIEST DEPARTURE TIMES

Busiest departure times are 5:00 A.M. to 9:00 A.M. daily. During busy departure times, drop passengers off at the Lower/Arrivals level. They can take the escalator or elevator up to departures.

SHUTTLE TO TERMINALS

LAX Shuttle A provides service between terminals every 10 minutes daily. Shuttle C provides service every 10 to 15 minutes between LAX terminals and Economy Parking Lot C.

FLYAWAY BUS

FlyAway Bus provides scheduled round-trip transportation between LAX and surrounding Los Angeles locations: Union Station (Downtown Los Angeles), Van Nuys (San Fernando Valley), Westwood (West Los Angeles/UCLA), Santa Monica, and Hollywood. Fares are between $8-$10, depending on route. Passengers leaving LAX can board buses on the Lower/Arrivals Level.

METRO LINE

Shuttle G provides transportation to and from the Metro Green Line Aviation Station with a valid TAP card.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for LAX are $30 for Central Terminal Parking Structures and $12 for Economy Parking Lot C.

RESERVATIONS

None of the LAX Parking Structures accept parking reservations.

SHORT-TERM PARKING

Terminal Area Parking Structures are available for $3 the first hour, and $2 every 30 minutes (or fraction thereof) after the first hour. Maximum rate of $30 daily. Parking for motorcycles is free in Terminal Area Parking Structures. Parking lot structures P3 and P2B are closest to the Virgin America ticket counter and gates at Terminal 3 of LAX.

LONG-TERM PARKING

Economy Parking Lot C is available for $4 per hour and a maximum of $12 daily. Lot C is located at West 96th St. and Sepulveda Blvd. Passengers can walk to the LAX City Bus Center from Parking Lot C. Transportation to all terminals from Lot C is available on LAX Shuttle C.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The LAX Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorists awaiting arrival flights for up to two hours. Located on the Northwest corner of 96th street, adjacent to Economy Parking Lot C. Vehicles must be attended at all times. No parking is allowed.

ACCESSIBILITY

For flight connections, use the lift-equipped LAX Shuttle A for service between terminals. If airline assistance is not available, contact the Parking Lot C wheelchair shuttle service at 310.646.6402 or 310.646.8021.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle charging stations are located in LAX Central Terminal Parking Structures 1 and 6 and in Economy Parking Lot C. Electricity is provided free of charge. Parking rates apply.

MOTORCYCLE

Parking for motorcycles is free inside the Central Terminal Area parking structures. When entering, drive carefully around the entrance gate arm and DO NOT take a ticket. Do not park in a space for a car. A motorcycle may be parked at the ends of rows, in corners, and in other spaces provided it does not block access to parked vehicles, handicapped spaces, Smarte Carte machines, passenger traffic, wheelchair paths, automobile traffic, doors, elevators, driveways, etc. Parking violations will be issued to all offenders. When exiting, drive carefully around the exit gate arms. Entering/exiting a lot via a sidewalk is not allowed.
