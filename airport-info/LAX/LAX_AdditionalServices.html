SWEET EXTRAS

PET PARK

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an LAX Pet Park. Located on the Lower/Arrivals Level of the Central Terminal area between Terminals 1-2 or at the southeast end of the Central Terminal Area near Terminals 7-8.

OBSERVATION DECK

Get an amazing 360-degree view of LAX in the Observation Deck of the LAX Theme Building. Open 8 A.M. to 5 P.M. on Saturdays and Sundays.

THEME BUILDING

Visit the iconic LAX Theme Building during your layover at LAX. And grab dinner with a view in the Observation Deck. A cafeteria on the ground level is open to the public 6 A.M. - 7 P.M. weekdays, and 7 A.M. - 3 P.M. on weekends.
