DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for SJD are $305 pesos for all parking lots for first 24 hs, $110 pesos from second to fifth day and $100 pesos after the sixth day. No credit or debit cards are accepted.

RESERVATIONS

No reservations accepted at any of SJD parking lots.
