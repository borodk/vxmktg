SHUTTLE

FLL provides free shuttle service to all terminals, rental center, and economy parking lot. Terminal drop-off and pick-up stops are located at each terminal on the lower level outside of the Baggage Claim. Bus shuttles operate 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

BROWARD COUNTY TRANSIT

Use Route 1 of Broward County Transit to reach FLL airport. BCT operates daily schedules. The bus stop is located at the Rental Car Center, Stop 7. Click here for FLL Route 1 service information.

TRI Rail

Tri-Rail (commuter train) provides service to Miami-Dade County, Broward County, and Palm Beach County. There are free shuttle buses from the FLL terminals to the Fort Lauderdale - Hollywood International Airport Station at Dania Beach. The station is just a few miles from the airport and a short ride on the shuttle bus.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for FLL are $15 for short-term parking, $7.5 for long-term Economy parking, and $21 for short-term Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the FLL Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available at Hibiscus Garage Level 2 and Palm Garage Level 1. Daily parking is available at Hibiscus Garage all Levels except 2 and Palm Garage Levels 3 and 4 for $15 maximum. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking for $7.5 per 24 hours. Economy lots is located on 2755 SW 42nd Street. Airport shuttle buses provide service within each lot. Click here for more information.

VALET

Valet parking is available $21 daily for All Terminals, Palm Garage, Hibiscus Garage and Cypress. Click here for hourly parking rates.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The FLL Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located in 600 Terminal Drive with an Easy access from I-595, US-1, and Griffin Road. Also if you exit any self-park lot within 15 minutes of entering and there is no charge for parking.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all FLL parking facilities.
