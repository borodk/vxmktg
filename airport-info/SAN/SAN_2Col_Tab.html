AMTRAK & COASTER

Amtrak Intercity and Coaster commuter trains link SAN airport with Los Angeles and Orange Counties, downtown San Diego and coastal North County and. Travelers headed to and from SAN airport can connect with Amtrak's Pacific Surfliner and the COASTER at Amtrak's Santa Fe Depot train station downtown by using Metropolitan Transit System Route 992, which operates between 5:00 a.m. and 11:30 p.m.

METRO BUS

The Metropolitan Transit System's Route 992 connects the Airport to downtown San Diego on. Stops at Terminals 1 and 2 as well as the Commuter Terminal, and operates between 5:00 a.m. and 11:30 p.m., every 15 minutes on weekdays and every 30 minutes on weekends and holidays. This frequent bus service connects with Trolley, COASTER and Amtrak stations and is wheelchair accessible.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for SAN are $30 for short-term domestic parking, $20 for long-term international parking, $13 for long-term Economy parking, and $38 for short-term Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the SAN Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available in Terminal Lots 1 & 2 for $30 maximum. Access by walk to terminals. Click here for hourly parking rates.

INTERNATIONAL

International Parking is available in Long Term Lot Harbor Drive. Access Terminals by dedicated lot Shuttle. Maximum rate of $20 for 24 hours. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Lot Pacific Highway for $13 per 24 hours. Economy lots are located on 3365 Admiral Boland Way. Airport shuttle buses provide service within each lot. Click here for hourly parking rates.

VALET

Valet parking is available $38 daily for Domestic Terminals 1 and 2 in Terminal Lots. Car wash, oil change & other services available. Click here for hourly parking rates.

BICYCLE

Free bicycle Lockers are available at the north side of the West Wing Building at 2320 Stillwater Road and east of Terminal 1 pedestrian bridge, along the fence line. A $25 refundable key deposit is required. In addition, bicycle racks at the airport are located at the following locations: Commuter Terminal Parking Lot 1 (east side) and directly below the Terminal 1 Pedestrian Bridge. In order to use these lockers, and other SANDAG bicycle lockers in the San Diego region, sign up here. The San Diego Regional Bike Map can help you plan a convenient bicycle route to or from the airport.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The SAN Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located on North Harbor Drive, at Rent-a-Car Access Road, just east of the Airport Authority Administrative Offices.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all SAN parking facilities. All airport shuttle buses are fully accessible. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at SAN.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Two electric vehicle charging stations are available at SAN Terminal Lots 1 & 2. Click here for more information about EV parking at SAN.
