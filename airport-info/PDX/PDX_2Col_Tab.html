CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

Curbside baggage is currently not available for Virgin America guests at PDX.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

SHUTTLE

PDX provides free shuttle service to the Main Terminal from economy parking lot. The lot has red and blue bus routes, which correspond to color-specific parking shelters. Bus shuttles operate 24 hours a day, seven days a week, every 7-9 minutes from 4:00 a.m. to midnight, and every 15 minutes from midnight to 4:00 a.m.

TRIMET

MAX Red Line train of Trimet Public Transit in Portland area gives service to airport from city center. Train runs about every 15 minutes, Service is less frequent in the early morning and evening. The trip to/from downtown Portland takes about 38 minutes and costs $2.50 for Adults 18–64, $1 for Honored Citizens and $1.25 for Youth. The MAX station and ticket machines are located near baggage claim on the lower level.

AIRPORT SHUTTLES

Several Shuttle companies give service to PDX airport. Click here for more details. The pickup area for taxis, town cars, long-haul shuttles, hotel vans and parking lot shuttle buses is located in the center section of the airport terminal's lower roadway on the baggage claim and departure level. Airporter shuttles, off-airport rental car shuttles and reserved vehicles are found in the section of the lower roadway closest to the garage.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for PDX are $27 for short-term parking, $21 - $10 for long-term Economy parking, and $30 for short-term Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the PDX Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available in Short-term Parking Garage adjacent to Main Terminal. A skybridge on level 4 and tunnels connect the garage to the terminal. Maximum rate of $27 for 24 hours Click here for hourly parking rates.

INTERNATIONAL

International Parking is available in Long-term Parking Garage, adjacent to Short-term Parking Garage. Tunnels and moving sidewalks connect the seven-story garage to the terminal. Maximum rate of $21 for 24 hours. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking Lot for $10 per 24 hours. Economy lot is located near I-205 off of Airport Way. The lot has red and blue bus routes, which correspond to color-specific parking shelters. Click here for hourly parking rates.

VALET

Golden Key Valet parking is available $30 daily for Main Terminals. The valet drop-off is located on the outer lane of the upper, ticketing/departures level. Click here for hourly parking rates.

BICYCLE

Free motorcycle/bicycle parking is available on the arrival level under the upper roadway. Parking is well marked. Motorcycle parking is not available in any of the parking garages or in the Economy or PDX employee lots.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The PDX Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located off of N.E. Airport Way and N.E. 82nd Avenue.

DISABILITY

Marked disabled parking spaces are located on each floor of the parking garage near the elevators. Disabled parking spaces are located next to the bus shelters in both the red and blue economy lots. Disabled parking is offered at a discounted daily maximum parking rate of $10 per day for vehicles displaying a valid state-issued disabled permit, placard or license plate. The disabled parking rate is available in the short-term garage, long-term garage or economy lot. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at PDX.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Six Electric vehicle charging stations are located on the fourth floor of the short-term parking garage near the south skybridge. Two stations are available for valet customers, and valet staff will perform the charge. Click here for more information about EV parking at PDX.
