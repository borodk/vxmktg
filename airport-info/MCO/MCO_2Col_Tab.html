CHECK-IN

Main Terminal

COUNTER HOURS

Ticket counter opens three hours before flight departure.

CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

TSA PRE CHECK

TSA Pre✓™ expedited security screening is available in Main Terminal at Center of checkpoint for gates 60-129 of MCO.

PROHIBITED ITEMS

Certain sharp objects, liquids, foods, and gifts are not prohibited past security checkpoints. Read the TSA’s Prohibited Items list for more information about prohibited items in carry-on and checked baggage.

SHUTTLE

MCO provides Free shuttle to/from both Terminal A and B (Level 1). Shuttles pick-up/drop-off at spaces A17-A18 (Terminal A) and B18-B19 (Terminal B) on Level 1. Shuttles operate to and from the terminal 24/7, approximately every 10 minutes Please allow time to transfer between OIA - North Park Place and Terminal A or B Disabled access is available for the shuttle service to/from the terminal.

LOCAL BUSES

Lynx local Buses stops are located in Terminal A, on the Ground Transportation Level (Level 1), at Commercial Lane spaces A38-A41. To downtown use Lynx bus lines 11 and 51. For International drive use Lynx bus line 42. For Florida Mall and SeaWorld use Lynx Bus Line 111. Click here for MCO fare and service information.

SUNRAIL

SunRail riders may now connect to Orlando International Airport (MCO) via the bus link to the Sand Lake Road SunRail station. The Lynx (local) bus stop is located in Terminal A, on the Ground Transportation Level (Level 1), at Commercial Lane spaces A38-A41.

AMTRAK

AMTRAK station is 12 miles away from MCO airport, can be accessed by Lynx bus line 11. More information here.

GREYHOUND

Greyhound station is 13 miles away from MCO airport, can be accessed by Lynx bus line 11. More information here.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for MCO are $17 for short-term domestic parking, $10 for long-term Economy parking, and $25 for short-term Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the MCO Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available at Terminal Parking Garages A and B and Terminal Top Parking. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking Lots Blue and Red for $10 per 24 hours. Blue economy lot is located along Bear Rd. north Terminals and Red economy lots is located on south access road south Terminals. Airport shuttle buses provide service within each lot. Click here for hourly parking rates.

VALET

Valet parking is available $25 daily for Terminals A, B, and for all garages. Click here for hourly parking rates.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The MCO Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located in the South Park Place lot - turn right after you enter.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all MCO parking facilities. All MCO airport shuttle buses are fully accessible. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at MCO.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Electric vehicle charging stations are available at MCO on Level two of A (Row A) and B (Row P) Garages. Parking rates apply. Click here for more information about EV parking at MCO.
