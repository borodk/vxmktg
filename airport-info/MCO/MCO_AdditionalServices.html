SWEET EXTRAS

ANIMAL RELIEF AREAS

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet at an MCO Animal Relief Area. Located on Level 2 of Terminals A and B. The “A” side area is located at the far East end of the building (to the right as you exit the building). The “B” side area is located at the far West end of the building (to the right as you exit the building).
