CITY BUS

The city buses from the Blanca Riviera City to Cancun ($40 pesos) leave the airport every 20 minutes between 5:30 am and 11:30 pm , and stop at the national and international terminals. A straight line drawn from the exit of the international arrivals (beyond all vans), reaches the small lot and box office decorated with a poster of Coca-Cola.

To get to the hotel zone of Cancun, take any bus with a sign saying "R1" , "Hotels " or "Zona Hotelera" on the windshield, they use Avenida Tulum to Coba Avenue, then take East on Coba avenue. The price of a single ticket is $7.50 Mexican pesos, but since many times there is no change often varies between $7 and $8 pesos. If you're looking to get to Puerto Juarez and Isla Mujeres Ferry, you will take a bus off of the 13 Route ("Puerto Juarez" or "Punta Sam", $7.50 Mexican pesos) to go north on Avenida Tulum. Some buses R1 make this trip as well; tickets cost $8.50 pesos.

GRAY LINE EXPRESS

Comfortable Grayline Express buses (or shared vans) leave the sidewalk in front of the international terminal approximately every 15 minutes travelling to the Hotel Zone, Cancun and Puerto Morelos City; They charge $195 pesos per person to destinations in Cancun, and $326 pesos to the hotels between Cancun and Puerto Morelos.

ADO BUSES

ADO buses charge $40 Mexican pesos to go downtown or $100 to go direct to Playa del Carmen. Riviera also has nine first-class express buses from the airport to Playa del Carmen between 7:00 am and 7:30 pm ($100 Mexican pesos, 45 minutes to an hour). The service is live and tickets are sold at a counter that on the international section of the airport.

TAXIS

Cancun taxis do not have meters and the rates are set, but you should always agree on a price before getting in; otherwise, you could end up paying for a "misunderstanding ". Airport taxi’s (up to four people) charge up to $450 pesos to the city or the Hotel Zone taxis if taken just opposite the airport. However, if you follow the access road to exit the airport, past the guardhouse traffic (a total of about 300 meters), often you can take a taxi out of the airport that will charge you much less (you can try for $80 to $100 Mexican pesos) as the driver is no longer subject to the expensive rates regulated by the airport.
