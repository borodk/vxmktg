SWEET EXTRAS

ANIMAL RELIEF AREA

Ruff ruff! Find relief for your pet an ORD Animal Relief Area. Located curbside of the Lower Level of Terminals 1, 2, 5. For access from Terminal 3, use the elevator between Door 3A and 3D and exit Door 3A of Lower Level/Baggage Claim.

HILTON CHICAGO O’HARE AIRPORT HOTEL

The Hilton Chicago O'Hare Airport Hotel is located at ORD within walking distance of Domestic Terminals 1,2, and 3. Service between the hotel and International Terminal 5 is provided by ATS. The hotel offers amenities such as a barber shop, fitness center, sports bar, shower facilities, conference and meeting rooms. Day membership to the Health Center is available. Click here for more hotel information.

ORD MEDICAL CENTER

Receive medical services before or after your flight from the O'Hare Urgent Care Clinic. The clinic is available Monday - Friday 8:00 A.M. - 5:00 P.M., weekends and holidays 9:00 A.M. - 5:00 P.M. Located in Terminal 2 of ORD past security on the ticketing level.

PUBLIC ART PROGRAM

The Chicago Public Art Program is featured at ORD in dozens of sculptures​, paintings, murals, and exhibits throughout the airport. Performances and exhibitions are scheduled throughout the year to enhance the traveler’s experience at ORD. Click here for more information.

SPA

You can find multiple spas and health facilities throughout ORD.

YOGA ROOM

Find your zen, post-security, before boarding your flight in the ORD Yoga Room. Open daily 6:00 A.M. - 10:00 P.M. Located in Terminal 3 on the Mezzanine Level of the Rotunda.
