CHECK-IN TIME

We recommend arriving 90 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights, and 120 minutes prior to departure for international flights. Terminal 3 of ORD is one of the busiest terminals. We recommend arriving 2-3 hours before flights 4:00 A.M. - 10:00 A.M. to accommodate traffic at TSA Checkpoint 9 of ORD.

CURBSIDE BAGGAGE HOURS

This service is not available.

CHECKED BAGGAGE

Checked baggage will be accepted up until 45 minutes prior to flight departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

CARRY-ON

Check-in without bags closes 30 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior for international flights.

SHUTTLE

ORD provides free shuttle service to all terminals, parking garages, and the Airport Transit System. All shuttles are located at the Bus/Shuttle Center on Level 1 of the Main Parking Garage between Elevator Centers 3 and 4. Bus shuttles operate 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

COACH USA

Coach Usa Tri State/United Limo offers hourly bus service between ORD and Midway International Airport (MDW). Coach USA also offers services between ORD and surrounding Chicago locations. Buses are located at the Bus/Shuttle Center on Level 1 of the Main Parking Garage. Tickets are available online to purchase. Click here for ORD fare and service information.

GO AIRPORT EXPRESS

Go Airport Express offers daily shuttle service between ORD to surrounding Chicago hotels and suburban neighborhoods. Shuttles depart every 10-15 minutes from the Baggage Claim area of Domestic and International Terminals 1E, 3F, 2D and 5E. Reservations are required for transportation to the airport. Shuttle service starts as early as 4:00 A.M. for hotels and 5:30 A.M. for suburban neighborhoods. Tickets are available online to purchase.

AIRPORT TRANSIT SYSTEM

Operating 24 hours a day, year-long ORD’s Airport Transit System (ATS) provides free service to all domestic and international terminals, parking facilities, the Kiss ‘n’ Fly drop-off point, and PACE Bus Stop. Airport shuttle buses provide transportation from remote Parking Lot E and Lot F to ATS stations.

CHICAGO TRANSIT AUTHORITY

Chicago Transit Authority (CTA) offers 24 hour train service daily between ORD and surrounding Chicago cities and suburban neighborhoods via CTA Blue Line. The Blue Line Station is located on the Lower Level of Terminal 2 at ORD. Connections to other CTA Lines are available via Blue Line.

METRA NORTH CENTRAL SERVICE

METRA North Central offers weekday train service from downtown Chicago to north central suburbs. Connect to METRA North Central for travel from ORD via the O’Hare Transfer Station. The Transfer Station is located adjacent to Parking Lot F at ORD. For service from the Transfer Station to all ORD terminals, use the “Parking Lot F” Airport Shuttle for connection to ATS.

PACE BUS SERVICE

Pace provides bus service throughout the Chicago suburbs and direct to ORD. Direct service to ORD is available via Route 250 and Route 330. Daily Route 250 service, and weekday and Saturday Route 330 service is located at ORD Kiss ‘n’ Fly drop-off/ATS station in Remote Parking Lot E. Access to CTA Purple Line is available via Route 250 to Davis Street Station. Access to CTA Yellow Line is available via Route 250 to Skokie Swift Station. Connections to Amtrak, METRA, Quarry Shopping Center, and Countryside Plaza are available via Route 330. Click here for more information about Pace bus services at ORD.

DAILY RATE

Daily parking rates for ORD are $35 for short-term domestic parking, $60 for short-term international parking, $14 - $17 for long-term Economy parking, and $54 for short-term Valet parking.

RESERVATIONS

None of the ORD Parking Facilities accept parking reservations.

DOMESTIC

Short-term hourly parking is available Level 1 of the Main Garage adjacent to Domestic Terminals 1, 2, 3. Daily parking is available Level 2-6 of the Main Garage and at Outside Parking Lots B and C for $35 maximum. Main Garage and Outside Parking Lots are not intended for extended overnight parking. Click here for hourly parking rates.

INTERNATIONAL

International Parking is available in Lot D, adjacent to International Terminal 5. International Parking Lot D is not intended for long-term parking. Maximum rate of $60 for 24 hours. Click here for hourly parking rates.

LONG-TERM

Long-term parking is available in Economy Parking Lot E and G for $14 per 24 hours, and $17 per 24 hours. Economy lots are located along Bessie Coleman Drive. Airport shuttle buses provide service within each lot. Service to all ORD terminals is provided by ATS at Economy Parking Lot E. Click here for hourly parking rates.

VALET

Valet parking is available $54 daily for Domestic Terminals 1, 2, 3 in the Main Garage. Click here for hourly parking rates.

BICYCLE

Outdoor bicycle parking is available at ATS station in Economy Parking Lot E. Use ATS for service to all domestic and international terminals from Lot E. Bicycle parking is also available at CTA stations. Use CTA Blue Line for service to ORD.

CELL PHONE WAITING LOT

The ORD Cell Phone Waiting Lot is available free to motorist awaiting arrival flights. Located north of Economy Parking Lot F. Use exit I-190 at North Mannheim Road to access Cell Phone Waiting Lot entrance (note: Economy Parking Lot F is closed as of 7/15/2015). Flight arrival information display available on location.

KISS 'N' FLY

Avoid ORD terminal congestion by dropping off loved ones at Kiss ‘n’ Fly. Located directly off Bessie Coleman Drive. Use International Terminal exit from I-190 to access Kiss ‘n’ Fly entrance. Service to all domestic and international terminals is provided by ATS at Kiss ‘n’ Fly drop off.

DISABILITY

Accessible parking is available at all ORD parking facilities. All ATS trains and airport shuttle buses are fully accessible. Click here for more information about accessibility services available at ORD.

ELECTRIC VEHICLE

Two electric vehicle charging stations are available at ORD on Level One of the Main Parking Garage, adjacent to the Bus/Shuttle Center entrance. Parking rates apply. Click here for more information about EV parking at ORD.
